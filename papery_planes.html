Papery Planes

Papery Planes lets you experience the joy of flying your own precious paper plane through

night & day, different seasons and environments.

 

Touch the screen left or right to guide your paper plane through a charming and atmospheric

world - but don't let yourself be tricked - there is no room for mistakes.

 

How far can you glide before you crumble to pieces ?
