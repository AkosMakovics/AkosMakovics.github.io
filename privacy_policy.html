General Application Privacy Statement

Akos Makovics provides this Privacy Policy to explain how we treat our user’s data.


Why do we collect data?


There are several reasons why we do that:
  • To improve our games by analyzing statistical, non-personalized data about events in our games.
  • To provide social experiences like leaderboards, tournaments, gifts, user ratings, user comments.
  • To show you ads and provide content for free.


What kind of data we collect?


Data that we collect and provide to our partners may include:
  • Unique advertising identificators (Google Advertising ID, IDFA).
  • Unique device identificators.
  • Anonymized IP address.
  • Gamer ID (Android).
  • Game Center ID (iOS).
  • IP address.


Analytics


Most of our apps use analytics (usage data) in order to understand how users engage with our apps and how to improve the overall experience. The information gathered include: the device type, the session length, interactions with various user interface elements in the game. The analytics partners that we work with are:
  • Unity Analytics: https://unity.com/solutions/analytics
  • Unity Analytics has their own privacy policies, please visit their websites to find more information.


Advertising


Some of our games use advertising to provide the content free of charge. Those games launched on your device automatically send certain information to our advertising partners. Those information include: the name of the app, the device type and the advertising ID. Our advertising partners are:
  • AdMob: https://www.google.com/admob
  • Unity Ads https://unity3d.com/unity/features/ads

Our partners may try to personalize the ads for you to provide you the ads that are more interesting to you. Our partners have their own privacy policies, please visit their websites to find more information.


Children’s information


We are concerned about protecting the privacy of children aged 16 or under, if we learns that personal data of users of 16 or under has been collected, other than for support of the videogame, we will remove such information. If you are aged 16 or under‚ please get your parent/guardian's permission beforehand whenever you provide us with personal information.


How to get your data removed?


If you would like to get all of your data removed, please contact us
